Race Across the Universe!

Ready to blast off on an excting, out-of-this-world adventure?!

In this game, you can experience the Solar System and the rest of the Universe firsthand and explore these celestial bodies. Be warned, this won't be an easy journey. There are many dangerous places near and far from the Sun and you never know what to expect. Fortunately, you can overcome these obstacles with the roll of a dice. Get rewards to upgrade your dice throughout the game. They may end up saving your life. So head down to the launch pad and blast off! Space Awaits!!!
