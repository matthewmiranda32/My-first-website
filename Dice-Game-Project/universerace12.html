Solar flares!!!

Just when you think you had it easy, some other conundrum pops up. You're right in the path of a solar flare! When storms brew on the Sun, the planets and interplanetary travelers can feel the effects of them appreciably. This storm will not only roast your craft, it'll cause damage to your instruments and disrupt your communications. I hope you have your six-sided die handy...

Roll a 6 to get your ship away from the Sun.
