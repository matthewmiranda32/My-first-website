Famous Volcanoes Around the World

Volcanoes are all over the world, but they typically form at the borders between Earth's tectonic plates. A plethora of volcanoes border the Pacific Ocean in what has come to be known as the Pacific Ring of Fire. More volcanoes are found elsewhere in the world. It would be impossible to list all the world's volcanoes on one page. So I'm giving you a list of the most famous ones.
